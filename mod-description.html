Attach it · Wear it · Show it off
Immersive Modular Backpacks
A backpack shouldn't be an invisible inventory slot you forget you're wearing.
Bolt on pouches, sacks, a toolstrap, or a lantern - extend your storage, light your way, or just show off your tools.
✔ Multiplayer synced 🔦 Working lantern light
Worn, not hidden. Attach something and you actually see it - on your character, in your hands, right there in the world. No hunting through the menu.
Build it your way. Slot on pouches, sacks, and straps; each one gives you more room to haul. Mix and match until the pack fits how you play.
More than decoration. Addons aren't just for show - a lantern lights your way, a toolstrap carries your tools.
Set it down. Place your pack as a block and it works like a chest - Ctrl + right-click to open it, right-click to pick the whole thing back up, addons and all.
Two packs to build on
Standard Backpack
7 attachment points
4 × Pouch
2 × Toolstrap
1 × Lantern or Shield
Sturdy Backpack
10 attachment points
6 × Pouch (2 also take a Sack)
3 × Toolstrap
1 × Lantern or Shield
Addons to bolt on
Pouch
+3 slots
A small bag in its own right - wear it in a bag slot, or fit it to a backpack point to add its slots to the pack.
Sturdy Pouch
+5 slots
A roomier pouch that fits the sturdy backpack's larger attachment points.
Tool Strap
Carries 1 tool
Holds a single tool. Fitted to a pack, the tool rides visibly on your back instead of vanishing into a slot.
Compatible with
✔Immersive Backpacks by Deven
✔Equus: Wild Horses - bags keep rendering on the seraph. For bags on horseback, add Modular Backpacks: Equus Compatibility.
✔Equus: Medieval Horses
✔KCs DragonFolk - requires Modular Backpacks: KCs DragonFolk Compatibility.
⚠Immersive Backpack Overhaul — its bags can't be attached to a backpack as addons.
✔Storage Tweaks — sort and stack work on a backpack, placed or worn. Tools stay on their toolstrap.
✔Roll-up bed — its mattresses strap to a sturdy backpack, just like the hay bed.
✔Player Model Library — bags and their addons render on custom player models, such as Rust Girls.
